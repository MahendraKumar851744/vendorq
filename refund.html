Refund & Cancellation Policy

FLUX SOLUTIONS — VendorQ Platform

This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines how refunds are handled for the VendorQ platform, operated by FLUX SOLUTIONS. We aim to be fair and transparent with all our users.

1. Subscription Refunds

  • VendorQ offers monthly subscription plans priced in INR.
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
  • A full refund of subscription fees is available if requested within 7 days of payment, provided the paid features have not been used during that period.
  • No refunds will be issued for partially used billing periods after the 7-day window.
  • To request a subscription refund, contact us at androiprogrammers@gmail.com.

2. Free Plan

The Starter (free) plan has no charges and therefore no refund applies. You may stop using it at any time without any obligation.

3. Food Order Payments

Important: VendorQ does not collect, hold, or process any customer payments for food orders. All UPI payments are made directly from the customer to the vendor's bank account. VendorQ acts solely as a technology platform facilitating the ordering process.

  • Refunds for food orders are the sole responsibility of the vendor.
  • Customers seeking a refund for a food order must contact the vendor directly.
  • VendorQ does not mediate payment disputes between customers and vendors.

4. Cancellation of Orders

  • Customers may request order cancellation by contacting the vendor directly before the order is prepared.
  • Once an order has been accepted and preparation has begun, cancellation is at the vendor's discretion.
  • Any refund for cancelled orders is handled directly by the vendor via UPI or other means.

5. Payment Failures & Technical Issues

  • If a UPI payment fails but the amount is debited, the refund will be processed automatically by the customer's bank or UPI app as per standard UPI guidelines (typically within 3-5 business days).
  • For persistent payment issues, customers should contact their bank or UPI app support.
  • For platform-related technical issues affecting payments, contact us at androiprogrammers@gmail.com.

6. Refund Processing

  • Approved subscription refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used for the subscription.
