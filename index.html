How It Works

Simple 4-step process for your customers

1

Scan QR Code

Customer scans the unique QR code at your stall using their phone camera.

2

Browse & Order

View your menu with prices in INR, add items to cart, and place the order.

3

Pay via UPI

Pay securely through PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, or any UPI app.

4

Track & Pickup

Get a queue number, track order status in real-time, and pick up when ready.

Online Order Management

Receive orders digitally on your vendor dashboard. Accept, prepare, and mark orders as ready - all from your phone or tablet. No more shouting order numbers.

Privacy Policy

VendorQ is committed to protecting your privacy. Here is how we handle your data:

  • Data Collected: We collect vendor business details (name, phone, UPI ID) and customer order details (name, phone number, order items).
  • Usage: Data is used solely for order processing, notifications, and generating sales reports for vendors.
  • Storage: All data is stored securely and is not shared with third parties except as required for service operation.
  • Customer Data: Customer phone numbers and names are collected only for order tracking and notifications. We do not use this data for marketing.
  • UPI Information: VendorQ does not store any UPI PINs, passwords, or bank account details. Payments are processed entirely through UPI apps.
