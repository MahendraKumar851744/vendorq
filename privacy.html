Privacy Policy

FLUX SOLUTIONS — VendorQ Platform

FLUX SOLUTIONS ("we", "our", "us") operates the VendorQ platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our services.

1. Information We Collect

From Vendors:

DataPurpose
Business nameDisplay on menu page and orders
Phone numberAccount verification and support
UPI IDGenerate payment links for customers
Menu items & pricesDisplay digital menu to customers
Location (optional)Help customers find the stall

From Customers:

DataPurpose
Name (optional)Order identification
Phone numberOrder notifications and status updates
Order detailsOrder processing and vendor dashboard

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect UPI PINs, passwords, or banking credentials.
  • We do not store credit card, debit card, or bank account numbers.
  • We do not process or hold any payment funds — all UPI payments go directly from customer to vendor.
  • We do not track customer location or device information beyond what is necessary for the web browser to function.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Order Processing: To facilitate orders between customers and vendors.
  • Notifications: To send order status updates to customers (e.g., order accepted, food ready).
  • Vendor Reports: To generate sales analytics and reports for vendors on their dashboard.
  • Customer Support: To respond to queries and resolve issues.
  • Platform Improvement: To improve our services based on aggregate, anonymized usage patterns.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be shared in the following circumstances:

  • With Vendors: Customer name and phone number are shared with the vendor solely for order fulfillment and notifications.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Service Providers: With trusted service providers who assist in operating our platform (e.g., hosting), bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored securely on protected servers.
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

6. Data Retention

  • Vendor data is retained as long as the vendor account is active.
  • Customer order data is retained for the purpose of vendor reports and analytics.
  • Upon account deletion request, all associated data will be removed within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and all associated data.
  • Opt-out: You can opt out of non-essential communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at androiprogrammers@gmail.com.

8. Cookies

VendorQ may use essential cookies to maintain session functionality. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

9. Children's Privacy

VendorQ is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of VendorQ after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
